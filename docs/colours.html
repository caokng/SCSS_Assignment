My Colour Swatches

These are my colours for my Design System. My Primary Palette will be used sparingly for small details and important pieces of information. My Secondary Palette will be used for the majority of components, such as backgrounds, buttons large text bodies. My tertiary palette will be for greyed out buttons and also background and unfilled form fields.
