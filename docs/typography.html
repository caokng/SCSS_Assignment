My Typography

This is my Typography Frame. My Headings will be used for the top of a text to draw attention. My body text will be for the bulk of the information used on the screen. My label will be used to name elements and draw attention and finally my captions will be for under images to provide context but not overwhelm the users view. The link typography will be used in bodies of text to stand out from the rest.
